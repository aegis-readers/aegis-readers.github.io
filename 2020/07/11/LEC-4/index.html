AEGIS W04-[Reading] Spectre Attacks Exploiting Speculative Execution

PoBu didn’t have time for his Shield Session, so AEGIS W04 is a Sword Session. In this webinar, I am going to provide an introduction on Paul Kocher’s S&P’19 paper Spectre Attacks: Exploiting Speculative Execution based on my reading. This is our first webinar on a serie of Security top-100 paper project. The paper reading webinar is different from previous one, and is audience based. After brief introduction of paper contents, we hold a discussion and a small brainstorm seminar. This is not going to be a traditional stand-up group meeting, and there is no need of being too tighty. So just relax!
