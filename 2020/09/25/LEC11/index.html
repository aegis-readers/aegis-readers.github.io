AEGIS W11-ZKP Introduction

In the 11th AEGIS Webinar, we are going to host our third introductory session (The previous two are wireless intro and SGX intro). In this talk, we invited Ye Zhang from New York University. He will give us a webinar introducing basic concepts and applications of ZKP. This is a introduction webinar, and anyones with any background are encouraged to participate. We will have 30 minutes of webinar and a 30 minute brainstorm discussion.

Time：

  • Sep 27 SUN, 1000-1100 [GMT+8]
  • Sep 26 SAT, 2100-2200 [CDT]

Topic：

  • ZKP Introduction
  • Talker: Ye Zhang@NYU
