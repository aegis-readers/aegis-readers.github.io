The 2nd AEGIS Symposium on Offensive and Defensive Security

2023 - 2024 (Online)

The Annual rEading Group in Information Science (AEGIS) Symposium is an annual symposium held online by a group of researchers of Privacy and Security in Computer Science.

In 2020, the 1st AEGIS Symposium hosted 16 talks given by a variety of security researchers in offensive and defensive security, covering a wide range of topics including Cryptography, System, Arch, Network, IoT, and AI security. The 2023 - 2024 symposium will be the 2nd symposium of the AEGIS symposia series.

The call for presentation and presenter nomination is up alive. Please nominate the presenters or the presentations you are most interested here. As usual, the 2nd AEGIS Symposium will be delivered online via Zoom. For any additional questions, please contact the organization committee.
