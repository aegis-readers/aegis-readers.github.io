About AEGIS Readers

Welcome aboard!

This is Advanced rEading Group in Information Security. We are a group of fresh PhD students interested in a variety of topics in Computer Science, with a special focus on Information Security. This group was founded in March, 2020, and we successfully arranged our first webinar in late June, 2020.

Hoping to improve our presentation skills and learning from outstanding fellows and peers, we try to introduce state-of-the-art (s.t.o.a.) papers, share our research works, and provide our systemizations of knowledge.

The webinar is arranged twice a week, in Sunday morning and Monday morning separately (GMT+8). The first session is the Sword Session that focus on Mobile Security and Computer Security. The second session is the Shield Session that focus on Computer System and Computer Archinecture. At each beginning of the week, talkers and topics will be decided and released, together with a back-up option in case the talker have other arrangement. After each webinar, the video will be recorded, and slides will be released in this website.

For any suggestions on the arrangements, topics, websites, please feel free to file an issue at our corresponding Github repository here.

P.S. AEGIS is the shield used by Zeus.
P.P.S. Mobile, Architecture, Security, and Systems can be denoted as M.A.S.S.
P.P.P.S. The official name of the webinar should be AEGIS M.A.S.S. Webinar.
