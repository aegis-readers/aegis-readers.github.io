Call for Presentations

The AEGIS Symposium is a symposium mainly for PhD students to share their researches, including but not limited to the published works, presentations for candicacy of dissertation, and any works you find proud of! Utilizing this symposium for practicing your future presentation is also highly encouraged!.

Since 2020, the AEGIS Symposium hosted 2 symposiums talks given by a variety of security researchers, covering a wide variety of topics including Cryptography, System, Arch, Network, IoT, Web, and LLM security.

The CFP stands for "Call For Presentation". Please nominate yourself or invite your friends (we strongly encourage!) you are interested in hearing from to apply as presenter here, or share the topics you want to hear in AEGIS 2024. The topic accepted by AEGIS widely spans from various security field, please just share with us what topic you would like to share to the AEGIS audiences!

If you are interested (or you have friends that may be interested in presenting), please fill out this form before 31th March, 23:59 pm AoE. See you in AEGIS'24!
